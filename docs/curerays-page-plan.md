## Page Responsibilities

- Dashboard: compact overview only; KPIs, urgent queue, pending signatures, overdue tasks, blocked courses, schedule, missing docs, treatment progress, and recent activity.
- Patients: chart-rounds-style master registry with phase, status, diagnosis, location, MD, staff, next action, flags, notes, summary, follow-up, and billing readiness.
- Patient Workspace: single patient/course hub with timeline and tabs for workflow, tasks, forms, planning, imaging, documents, delivery, billing/audit, and activity.
- Workflow: Carepath steps 0-14 as structured workflow rows.
- Tasks: role-based queues for my tasks, team tasks, unassigned, signatures, overdue, and completed.
- Schedule: appointments and treatment timing linked to workflow steps.
- Clinical Forms: structured template engine, starting with Hand Arthritis X-ray Mapping.
- Treatment Planning: IGSRT / skin planning parameters, review state, prescription state, and isodose output placeholder.
- Imaging: image categories, upload metadata, phase/fraction links, and audit evidence.
- Treatment Delivery: active treatment queue and fractionation table.
- Documents: template library, generated documents, versions, signatures, exports, and eCW upload placeholders.
- Billing / Coding: planned/completed/billed quantities and documentation readiness.
- Audit & QA: final course closeout validation.
- Analytics / Reports: operational reporting and bottleneck insight.
- Settings: workflow, template, storage, dropdown, notification, billing, and security configuration.
- Security Logs: sensitive action trail for patient/course/document/signature/file/audit events.
