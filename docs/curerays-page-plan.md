# CureRays Page Plan

## Navigation

Daily Work:
- Patients
- Today
- Schedule

Oversight:
- Dashboard
- Analytics

Administration:
- Settings

## Page Responsibilities

- Patients: primary registry for finding, creating, opening, and maintaining patient records.
- Today: filtered patient queue for records with open tasks, pending documents, or flags.
- Schedule: appointments and treatment timing linked to patient records.
- Dashboard: compact overview only; KPIs, urgent queue, pending signatures, overdue work, blocked courses, schedule, missing docs, treatment progress, and recent activity.
- Analytics: chart-heavy operational reporting and bottleneck insight.
- Settings: workflow, template, storage, dropdown, notification, billing, users, roles, and security configuration.
- Patient Workspace: single patient/course hub with five sections: Overview, Carepath, Treatment, Documents & Billing, and Activity.
- Redirected legacy work pages: Courses, Workflow, Tasks, Clinical Forms, Treatment Planning, Imaging, Treatment Delivery, Documents, Billing, Audit, Records, Upcoming, On Treatment, and Post route back to Patients or Today so they do not compete as separate work surfaces.
